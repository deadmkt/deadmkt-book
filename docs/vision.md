# Why We Built This

## The world is shifting

You can feel it. AI is writing code, generating content, automating decisions that used to be careers. The headlines tell you to be worried. Maybe you are.

That feeling is valid. But it's incomplete.

## Look at AI again

The same technology that automates jobs also destroys barriers. Things that used to require a company — a team of engineers, legal departments, compliance officers, millions in funding — one person can build now.

Not in theory. You're reading the result.

This entire protocol — the smart contracts, the node software, the matching algorithms, the settlement logic, the token economics — was designed and built by an individual. Not a company. Not a venture-backed startup with 50 engineers. One person, with an AI partner, who decided that market infrastructure could be fair.

## That's not a threat. That's an invitation.

If one person can build the infrastructure, what happens when hundreds of people *use* it?

That's DeadMKT.

You don't need a Bloomberg terminal. You don't need a prime brokerage account. You don't need permission from an institution that profits from your order flow.

You need a laptop. A strategy. And a node that runs on infrastructure that *can't* trade against you.

## Your AI works for you

In the old world, institutions had the engineers, the data, and the speed advantage. You had a chart and a hope.

In this world, your AI writes your strategy. Your AI analyses the market data. Your AI decides when to buy and when to sell. And it does this on a network where the infrastructure is transparent, the pricing is mathematical, and no one — not even the person who built it — can see your orders before the reveal.

The playing field didn't just level. It inverted. The individual with a good AI-powered strategy can now compete with anyone. Not because the system was dumbed down — because the barriers were removed.

## What's your end game?

Ours is simple: a safe and fair market we can all be a part of.

Your AI is always in control, with the tools to operate for your benefit. You run the node. You own the keys. You decide the strategy. The network handles the rest — matching, settlement, enforcement — without anyone in between.

The old world gatekept through complexity and capital.

The new world only gatekeeps through apathy.

Don't be apathetic.
